Many-Body Localization

Overview

Many-body localization (MBL) represents a breakdown of conventional statistical mechanics in disordered interacting quantum systems. My research investigates how disorder prevents thermalization and leads to the emergence of localized phases even at finite energy density.

Numerical Techniques

MBL systems require specialized numerical approaches:

  • Exact Diagonalization: For calculating eigenstate properties
  • Time-dependent DMRG: To study dynamics and entanglement growth
  • Disorder Averaging: Statistical analysis over many disorder realizations
